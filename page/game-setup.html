WBase Nibbles
Copyright (C) Geeshow Corporation 2020 Nibbles is a game for one or two players. Navigate your snakes around the game board trying to eat up numbers while avoiding running into walls or other snakes. The more numbers you eat up, the more points you gain and the longer your snake becomes. Game Controls
